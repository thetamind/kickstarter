<h1> </h1>
<h1>CUSTOMIZE BY CODING</h1>
<p>Want your watch to tell you when your next bus is leaving? Maybe you're jonesing to see your compile status or recent github commits.. Think push notifications, directly to your watch using the data connection on your phone. Want to check-in on your watch, or create an app that can monitor your sleep? Pebble can send data from the accelerometer and buttons back up to the internet. </p>
<p>Pebble can receive simple alerts and notifications from <b><i>if this then that</i> (ifttt.com) </b>or our web-facing RESTful endpoint. More adventurous developers can use the Pebble SDK, with its Arduino-like abstractions and simple C structure, to gain full control of the watch. Multiple apps can run on Pebble, along side watchfaces and regular notifications.</p>
<ul>
<li>
<b>Load apps using Bluetooth</b> </li>
<li>
<b>
144 x 168 pixel display</b> black and white e-paper</li>
<li>
<b>
Bluetooth</b> 2.1+ EDR and 4.0 (Low Energy)</li>
<li><b>4 buttons</b></li>
<li><b>Vibrating motor</b></li>
<li>
<b>
3 axis accelerometer</b> with gesture detection</li>

<h1><b>How does it work?</b></h1>
<p>
Pebble connects by Bluetooth to your iPhone or Android device. Setting up Pebble is as easy as downloading the Pebble app onto your phone. All software updates are wirelessly transmitted to your Pebble.</p>
<p><i>Compatibility</i></p>

<ul>
<li>
<b>iPhone 3GS, 4, 4S running iOS 5 </b>or<b> any iPod Touch with iOS 5.</b>
</li>
<li>
<b>Android devices running OS 2.3 and up</b>. Works great with Android 4.0 (Ice Cream Sandwich)!</li>
</ul>
<p>Unfortunately Pebble does not work with Blackberry, Windows Phone 7, or Palm phones at this time.</p>

<h1><b>FAQ</b></h1>
<p><b>Q. How long does the battery last?    A.</b> The rechargeable battery keeps Pebble going for 7+ days. </p>
<p><b>Q. Does the watch display stay on all the time?    A. </b>Yes. The display shows a watchface at all times.</p>
<p><b>Q. Does it have a backlight?    A. </b>Yes, Pebble has a backlight to view the display at night.</p>
<p><b>Q. Is it waterproof?    A. </b>Pebble is now water resistant, at least enough to go swimming, run in the rain etc. You can fully submerge Pebble in water. We're working on getting the actual certification level settled (3ATM, 5ATM, etc). This is in response to your suggestions!</p>
<p><b>Q. Scratchproof?    A. </b>Yes, Pebble has a scratch and shatter resistant lens with an anti-glare optical coating.</p>
<p><b>Q. Does it use up my phones battery?    A. </b>Pebble is a Bluetooth device, similar to a Bluetooth headset. It does affect the battery life of your phone, usually using up an extra 5-10% per day.</p>
<p><b>Q. How does it charge?    A.</b> We include a special USB cable. You can charge Pebble using your computer or any wall USB charger.</p>
<p><b>Q. Can I replace the bands?</b> <b>A.</b> Yes, Pebble is designed to fit most 22mm watch bands.</p>
